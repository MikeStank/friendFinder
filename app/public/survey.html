Maybe It'll Help... Most Likely Not

The dating site for people that don't like kids on their lawn and find that Steve from down the street is a little too nice all of the time (he thinks he's SO cool).

On a scale of 1-5, determine where you stand on each of the 10 questions below. 0 is you have never, and would never, find yourself in a similar situation. 5 is is you completely relate, and that's the problem.

  1. Question 1:

    When someone chews loudly, you feel like you'd rather have someone stab your eardrums with pencils.

  2. Question 2:

    You find yourself questioning your existence about every 5 to 10 minutes and each time makes you feel a little worse than before.

  3. Question 3:

    When the dog won't stop barking, the most acceptable and sure-fire way to make them stop is to bark back as loud as possible.

  4. Question 4:

    You don't have many pet-peeves, but you really don't like it when people use their pencil eraser too loudly.

  5. Question 5:

    Every morning when you wake up, the first words out of your mouth are astonishingly vulgar and offensive.

  6. Question 6:

    It'd be totally fine if people slurped when they ate cereal or soup, as long as I could shoot spit wads at them while they did it.

  7. Question 7:

    Although you enjoy peoples' company, you'd rather not...

  8. Question 8:

    The thought of your roommate from 6 years ago eating the frozen pizza that you specifically told them not to eat, still bothers you from time to time.

  9. Question 9:

    People that go "AHHHHHHHHHH" after every drink shouldn't be able to drink in front of people.
